About SVNHS

Signal Village National High School (SVNHS) is a dynamic educational institution located in Taguig City, Metro Manila, Philippines. We are dedicated to providing quality education and fostering a nurturing environment where students can grow intellectually, socially, and morally. Our school offers a comprehensive curriculum designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to succeed in the modern world. At SVNHS, we believe in the power of education to transform lives and communities. Our dedicated faculty, staff, and administrators work hand in hand with students and parents to ensure that every learner is supported and empowered to reach their full potential. Join us as we continue to build a strong foundation for the future, one student at a time.

Mission

Extra Curricular Activities

Heralds PepSquad

Heralds is a cheerleading squad, likely involved in boosting school spirit, supporting athletic teams, and entertaining audiences with performances that combine gymnastics, dance, and cheers. They motivate and energize crowds during sports events and school functions.

Indayaw

Indayaw refers to a group or ensemble that performs traditional Filipino folk dances in school settings. These dances are an important part of cultural preservation, showcasing the rich heritage and history of the Philippines through rhythmic movements, music, and costumes.

Quiz Bees

A quiz bee is a competitive event where participants answer a series of questions across various subjects, often testing general knowledge, academic skills, or subject-specific expertise. These events encourage learning and intellectual growth among students.

Leadership Excellence

Leadership refers to the ability to guide, inspire, and motivate others towards a common goal. In a school context, it involves the skills and qualities that enable a student or teacher to influence others, make decisions, and create a positive, productive environment. Leadership is often showcased through roles in clubs, sports teams, and other school organizations.
